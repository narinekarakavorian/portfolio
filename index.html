My services

Fashion Design

As a Fashion Designer, I specialize in creating custom clothing, garments, accessories, and jewelry. My process involves a personalized approach where I work closely with clients to bring their unique vision to life. Whether it’s a bespoke dress for a special occasion, a one-of-a-kind accessory to complement an outfit, or a complete collection, I handle everything from initial sketches to the final product. My services include: Custom garment design, accessory and jewelry creation and bespoke fashion pieces tailored to your style and needs.

Styling

As a Stylist, I offer comprehensive styling services to enhance your personal style and ensure you look your best for any occasion. My services are versatile and can be tailored to suit different needs, including:
• Closet Styling: I help you organize and optimize your wardrobe, creating cohesive looks by matching and combining what you already own.
• Photoshoot Styling: Whether it’s for a fashion campaign, editorial shoot, or personal photoshoot, I provide styling expertise to ensure the final images are visually stunning and on-brand.
• Project Styling: For any special project or event, I curate outfits and styles that align with the theme and objectives, ensuring a polished and professional appearance.

Artistic Direction

As an Artistic Director, I guide the creative vision and execution of fashion-related projects. My role involves developing a coherent aesthetic and ensuring that every element aligns with the desired brand image or artistic concept. Services include:
• Brand Development: Crafting a distinct visual identity and narrative for your brand, including logo design, color schemes, and overall aesthetic direction.
• Creative Campaigns: Planning and directing fashion campaigns, from concept to completion, ensuring consistency and impact across all media.
• Mood Board Creation: Developing detailed mood boards to guide photo shoots, marketing materials, and other creative projects, ensuring a unified and compelling visual story.

Image Consulting

As an Image Consultant, I focus on helping clients enhance their personal and professional image through tailored advice and guidance. My goal is to boost your confidence and ensure you present yourself in the best possible light. Services include:
• Personal Style Assessment: Analyzing your current style and providing recommendations to elevate your overall look, including wardrobe updates and styling tips.
• Professional Image Development: Assisting with professional attire and grooming to ensure you make a positive impression in your career.
• Event Preparation: Offering personalized advice for special events, ensuring you look and feel your best for any occasion.
